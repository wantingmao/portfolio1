Apple Airpods Case Redesign

@aproduct.aday


Apple's Current Design

Pain Points

  • Detailed battery life is not shown
  • only displayed in green and amber dots
  • inconvenient to check battery life, user has to open smart device in order to view
  • Small colored light is not accessible for sight or color challenged individuals

Other methods available to users:

Iphone Widget:

  • Pros: easy to understand and offers clear information
  • Cons: many steps that users have to take to get there

Steps taken to view widget:

  1. Pick up device
  2. Unlock device
  3. Swipe to the page that contains the widget

Pop-up

  • Pros: easy to understand and offers clear information
  • Cons: several steps, sometimes pop-up will not appear if the user misses a step

Steps taken to view pop-up:

  1. Put airpods back into case
  2. Close the lid
  3. Move the case near device
  4. Open case
  5. Wait…
  6. Battery level is displayed

How can I design something that indicates battery life in an accessible way but is also compliant with Apple’s aesthetics?

Ideation

  • Apply pros of Apple's existing design
  • Minimize steps taken to check battery life
  • Display should accommodate sight/color challenged individuals
