Ecoeye

A browser extension for sustainable shopping

❮ ❯

Problem Statement

If only there was an easier and cheaper way to shop sustainably, then consumers could reduce their carbon footprint, hence help the environment which would lead to more confidence and positive emotions in their purchase. With more than 260 million digital shoppers in the U.S. as of 2021, there is a clear opportunity to meaningfully impact a huge number of people.

Research + Analysis

How consumerism affects the planet

  • Production, processing, and consumption are depleting the Earth's natural resources and water supplies
  • Frequent disposal of cheaply manufactured goods accounts for the plethora of trash destroying our ecosystem.
  • Factory operations create toxic byproducts while the use of commodities themselves creates pollutants and waste

Increase in online consumption

  • As of October 2018, 75% of online shoppers worldwide are making purchases at least once a month, and in 2021, there were over 260 million digital buyers in the U.S. alone.

Why?

  • A majority of companies now use online websites -> e-commerce websites
  • Faster access to products and services -> efficiency and convenience

Competitive Analysis

  • Good On You is a fashion ratings app and website that provides a directory of ethical clothing brands and information on supply chain transparency, commitment to animal welfare, and sustainability policies.
  • TreeClicks is a browser extension that receives an ad fee while you are shopping and invests in tree planting projects, with this ad fee
  • EcoCRED is a green lifestyle app that helps users lower their carbon footprint by helping them track the emissions of daily lifestyle choices.
  • thredUP is an online consignment and thrift store where you can buy and sell secondhand clothes.

Solution Statement

For digital shoppers who find it difficult to shop sustainably due to the heavy research that is involved in company verification and product alternatives, a browser extension that indicates verified sustainable brands on e-commerce websites, suggests similar but sustainable alternatives to a product, and provides incentives with cash-back rewards, would help them save time, energy, and encourage them to shop sustainably.
